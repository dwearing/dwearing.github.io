Fun Facts

  • Virginia is called the "Old Dominion state" likely because it was the first of England's overseas dominions.
  • The slogan "Virginia is for lovers" began with the slogan "Virginia is for history lovers." It was then adapted to be other things like "beach lovers" or "mountain lovers" and so on.
  • Virginia's state bird is the northern cardinal.
