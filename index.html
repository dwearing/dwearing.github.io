Virginia: All You Need to Know

  • Virginia is one of the original 13 colonies
  • Virginia's population is 8.64 million (12th highest in the country)
  • Eight presidents have been from Virginia, the most of any state
  • England's first American colony, Jamestown, is located in Virginia
  • Virginia is one of only two states that borders Washington D.C.
